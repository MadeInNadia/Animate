Wonder Egg Priority (Japanese: ワンダーエッグ・プライオリティ, Hepburn: Wandā Eggu Puraioriti) is an original Japanese anime television series animated by CloverWorks. The series is a co-production between Aniplex, NTV, and D.N. Dream Partners, and is written by Shinji Nojima, directed by Shin Wakabayashi, and features character designs by Saki Takahashi. DE DE MOUSE and Mito are composing the series' music. It premiered on NTV and other channels on January 13, 2021.[1] Kanata Aikawa, Tomori Kusunoki, Shuka Saitō, and Hinaki Yano perform the ending theme song "Life is Cider" (Life is サイダー, "Raifu Izu Saidā").[2][3]

Funimation licensed the series and will stream it on its website in North America and the British Isles, in Europe through Wakanim, and in Australia and New Zealand through AnimeLab.[4] Bilibili is streaming the series in Southeast Asia.[5]

Character[edit]

Ai Ohto (大戸アイ, Ōto Ai)
Voiced by: Kanata Aikawa[6]
She is a distinct 14 year old girl with heterochromia, because of which she is often bullied. She being the main character of the story around whom mysterious events occur. She is shown to skip school for certain reasons.
